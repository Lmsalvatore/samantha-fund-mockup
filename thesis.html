The Italian Tech Opportunity

Italy's talent, creativity and higher education form a healthy ecosystem for technology innovation. Italy's venture ecosystem is still largely untapped: valued at only 0.06% of GDP, compared to 0.4% in the US, it represents a significant whitespace for early-stage technology builders.

Samantha.Fund focuses on technology opportunities within market verticals where Made in Italy has a proven competitive advantage — food, fashion, design, biotech and automation — leveraging deep local market knowledge and a wide business network to identify promising deal flow.
