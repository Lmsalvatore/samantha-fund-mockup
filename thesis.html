The Opportunity

The Italian Tech Opportunity — 2026

The generative AI wave, the EU AI Act, and the acceleration of the energy transition have created a structural inflection point for European tech. Italy's early-stage ecosystem — valued at roughly 0.06% of GDP vs. 0.4% in the US — remains one of the most underserved in the G7.

Samantha.Fund focuses on technology opportunities where Made in Italy has a structural edge: the intersection of AI, tokenisation, and longevity biotech with sectors like food, fashion, design, and advanced manufacturing.

The EU AI Act creates a compliance-first opportunity for Italian builders; PNRR infrastructure unlocks digital adoption across the supply chain.
